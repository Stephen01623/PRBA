What is Rollball?

Rollball is a fast-paced, exciting sport that combines elements of basketball, handball, and skating. It is played on roller skates, making it a unique and dynamic game that requires agility, coordination, and teamwork.

About Us

The Philippine Rollball Association (PRBA) is dedicated to promoting and developing the sport of rollball in the Philippines. Our mission is to empower youth through sports, fostering discipline, teamwork, and a sense of community.

We organize training programs, tournaments, and events to nurture talent and encourage participation at all levels. Join us in our journey to make rollball a thriving sport in the country!
